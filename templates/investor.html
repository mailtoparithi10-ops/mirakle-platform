FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the minimum investment amount?

There is no platform minimum. Investment amounts vary by startup and funding round, typically ranging from $25K to $5M+ per deal.

How are startups vetted?

Our team conducts preliminary due diligence including background checks, financial review, market validation, and team assessment. Only 3% of applicants are accepted.

What are the fees?

Platform access is free for qualified investors. We charge a 2% carry on successful exits to align our interests with yours.

Can I co-invest with other investors?

Yes! We facilitate syndicate investments and connect you with other investors for larger rounds or shared due diligence.

How do I get started?

Click "Application Form" above to submit your application. Our team will review and grant access within 48 hours for qualified investors.
